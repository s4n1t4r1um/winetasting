About Cabernet Sauvignon

Cabernet Sauvignon is one of the world's most widely recognized red wine grape varieties. Originating from the Bordeaux region of France, it is renowned for its depth of flavor, high tannin content, and excellent aging potential. Wines made from Cabernet Sauvignon often exhibit notes of dark fruits like blackcurrant and plum, complemented by hints of oak, vanilla, and spice. This grape thrives in various climates, producing robust and structured wines that are a favorite among wine enthusiasts globally.
